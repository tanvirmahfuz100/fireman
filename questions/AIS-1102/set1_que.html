University of Barishal

Department of Accounting & Information Systems

Course: AIS 1102 Introduction to Business

Session: 2022-23, Year: 1st, Semester: 1st

Exam Type: Semester Exam, Total Marks: 60

1. Answer the following:

(a) Explain the concepts of revenue, costs, and profit. What are the five factors of production?

(b) Why is capitalism growing, socialism declining, and capitalism still popular?

(c) What is the difference between demand-pull and cost-push inflation?

(d) List the components of the internal and external business environments.

(e) Define and contrast perfect competition and pure monopoly. Why is it hard to find perfect competition?

2. Answer the following:

(a) Describe the purpose and role of the WTO. What are the roles of the World Bank and the IMF in world trade?

(b) Explain the concept of countertrade. Discuss several ways that a company can enter international trade.

(c) Describe several cultural factors that a company involved in international trade should consider.

(d) What is a sole proprietorship? What are the advantages and disadvantages of a sole proprietorship?

(e) Differentiate between a private limited company and a public limited company.

3. Answer the following:

(a) What do you mean by Corporate Social Responsibility? Point out the areas of corporate social responsibility.

(b) Argue for or against corporate social responsibility.

(c) What is the role of top management in organizational ethics? Give an example of legal but responsible behavior.

4. Answer the following:

(a) Discuss the concept of natural trade barriers. Describe several tariff and non-tariff barriers to trade.

(b) Summarize the advantages and disadvantages of corporations. Which features contribute to the dominance of corporations in the business world?

(c) Differentiate between a merger and an acquisition. Describe the different types of corporate mergers.

(d) Define the term management. What are the four key functions of managers? What is the difference between efficiency and effectiveness?

(e) What is the purpose of planning, and what is needed to do it effectively? Identify the unique characteristics of each type of planning.

(f) What is the managerial pyramid? Explain the managerial function of organizing.

(g) Describe the control process. How can managers empower employees?

(h) What are the three types of managerial roles? Give examples of things managers might do when acting in each of the different roles.

(i) Define the basic managerial skills. How important is each of these skill sets at the different levels of the management pyramid?
