University of Barishal

Department of Accounting & Information Systems

Course: AIS 1101 Introduction to Business

Session: 2018-2019, Year: 2019, Semester: 1st

Exam Type: Semester Exam, Total Marks: 60

1. Define business.

2. “The ultimate objective of business is to earn profit.” Do you agree? Explain the objectives of business.

3. Explain the functional classification of business with appropriate examples.

4. “At present, business is required for the survival of human beings.” Do you agree? Explain.

5. What is the importance of international trade?

6. Discuss absolute and comparative advantages and how they impact international trade.

7. Define international trade. What are its key features?

8. What is monetary policy? How does it affect business operations?

9. Define fiscal policy. Discuss its implications in business decision-making.

10. Discuss the recent industrial policy in the country.

11.

a) Discuss the import and export policies of the government.
b) Define ethics and discuss its implications in business.
c) “Ethical business will grow, prosper, and have social acceptance.” Do you agree with this statement? Justify your answer.

12.

a) What do you mean by social responsibility? How can businesses perform this responsibility towards society?
b) What is business ethics? Explain the factors influencing ethical behavior.
c) What do you mean by an ethical dilemma? Explain ways to solve ethical dilemmas.

13. Write short notes on the following:

i) WTO (World Trade Organization)
ii) GATT (General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade)
iii) IMF (International Monetary Fund)
iv) DSE (Dhaka Stock Exchange)
v) BOI (Board of Investment)
